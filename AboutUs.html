History

The roots of Harris-Landscaping operations are personal.
Harris-Landscaping started in 2014 when Shoeb Harris relocated to Kingston Jamaica from the Cool Parish of St. Ann.
Mr. Harris was a young man with a vision to transform spaces into a comfortable, relaxed environment.
As a boy he followed behind his grand-father’s donkey to the family’s farm where he learnt about soil texture and treatment.
Upon Moving to Kingston he invested in a lawn mower and lined up neighbors as his first clients.
Formally launching Harris-Landscaping in the early 2015, Mr. Harris designed the business around aspirations;
creating beautiful natural surroundings and building lasting creative relationships with his clients.
